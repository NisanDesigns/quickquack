Meet the Author

Selda Nisanoglu

Mother of one daughter and an admirer of nature, Selda Nisanoglu received a master’s degree in fine art and is a Textile Designer. She aims to inspire future generations to cherish our Earth and every living thing on it. This book was inspired by the author’s workplace, which is home to a variety of stunning animals.
